  • Format 5v5
WePlay Pushka League is an online tournament organized by WePlay!, featuring 14 teams from two regions, Europe and CIS with a prize pool of $250,000 USD. So, there are seven teams per region, who will compete in the BO3 series in a round-robin format. After the rounds are done, three worst teams get eliminated, while four best crews continue to the playoffs with a double-elimination bracket, usual BO3. Pushka League ends in the Grand Finals, which will be played up to three wins (best of 5).
Sponsor: Weplay
